Dr. Maria Santos, DMD

Doctor of Dental Medicine

Licensed General Dentist – Philippines

Dr. Maria Santos has over 10 years of clinical experience in general, cosmetic, and restorative dentistry. She earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from a reputable Philippine university and continuously attends seminars and advanced training programs to stay updated with the latest dental technologies and techniques.

Her mission is to provide gentle, honest, and patient-centered care. Dr. Santos believes that educating patients and focusing on preventive dentistry are key to achieving long-lasting oral health.
