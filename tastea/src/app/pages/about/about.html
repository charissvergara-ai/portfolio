Our Story

Tastea was born from a simple idea: that a great cup of tea can change the course of your day. Founded by Mei Lin Chen in the heart of Portland, Oregon, our tea house started as a small corner shop where neighbors gathered for hand-brewed teas and heartfelt conversations.

What began with a curated selection of loose-leaf teas quickly grew to include artisan coffees and freshly baked pastries — each crafted with the same care and attention to quality that defines everything we do. Today, Tastea is a beloved neighborhood destination where the aroma of brewing tea mingles with the warmth of genuine hospitality.

We travel the world to source our ingredients — from the misty highlands of Darjeeling to the volcanic soils of Guatemala — because we believe exceptional drinks start with exceptional ingredients. Every cup we serve reflects our dedication to sustainability, authenticity, and the art of slow living.
