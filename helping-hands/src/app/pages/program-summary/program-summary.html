The Helping Hands Difference

End-to-End Support for Every Child

Unlike many organizations that only fund surgery, Helping Hands provides comprehensive support at every stage of a child's journey. We find children who would otherwise never receive help, prepare them physically and emotionally for surgery, cover every expense, and remain in their lives for years afterward.

Our team in the Philippines builds lasting relationships with the families we serve. We know every child by name, visit their homes, and celebrate their milestones. This is not just a program — it's a family.
