Our Mission

Filling the gap so Filipino children can receive cleft lip and palate surgery for free.

Where It All Began
How It Started

A Moment That Changed Everything

In 2011, our founder encountered a poor Filipino woman holding a baby boy with a cleft lip. The family desperately wanted surgery for their child but couldn't afford it. That heartbreaking moment ignited a mission that has since transformed hundreds of lives.

What began as one person's determination to help has grown into a comprehensive program that finds children in need, prepares them for surgery, funds their procedures, and supports them for years afterward.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end support for Filipino children born with cleft lip and palate.

Outreach

Our team in the Philippines travels to municipal offices, clinics, and neighborhood authorities to find children who need surgery.

Medical Pre-Screening

We visit families, document their needs, and provide medical screenings to determine if children are ready for surgery.

Nutritional Support

Many children are malnourished and ineligible for surgery. We provide nutritional assistance to prepare them for their procedure.

Transportation & Lodging

We cover all travel expenses and provide lodging and food during medical missions that may take several days.

Surgery Funding

Working with partner organizations, we ensure that families receive completely free cleft repair surgery.

Follow-up Care

After surgery, we maintain contact for several years, helping with medicine, follow-up visits, education, and living conditions.

Our Values

Compassion

Every child deserves a chance at a normal, healthy life regardless of their family's financial situation.

Integrity

100% of sponsorship funds are used to directly support our beneficiaries in the Philippines.

Community

We build lasting relationships with the families we serve and the communities they belong to.
