Our Mission

Filling the Gap for Children with Cleft

In 2011, our founder was moved by a poor Filipino woman holding a baby boy with a cleft lip whose family couldn't afford the surgery he needed. That moment sparked a mission to ensure no child should be denied a normal life because of poverty.

Helping Hands bridges the gap so that impoverished Filipino children can receive cleft lip and palate surgery completely free of charge. We handle everything — finding children, pre-screening, nutrition, transportation, lodging, and years of follow-up care.
