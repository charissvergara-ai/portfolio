How a Mission Works

Months of preparation go into every medical mission to ensure each child receives safe, effective care.

1

Planning

We coordinate with our surgical partner to select dates, locations, and the number of patients for each mission.

2

Patient Preparation

Our Philippines team identifies, screens, and prepares children, ensuring they meet health and weight requirements.

3

Mission Week

Volunteer surgical teams arrive, and over several days, children receive life-changing cleft repair surgery at no cost.

4

Follow-Up

After the mission, our team monitors each child's recovery, providing medicine and follow-up visits for years to come.
